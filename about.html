About

I have 18 years experience working in community mental health. Working closely with professionals and families to provide goal driven, individual, long term support.

I emphasize developing rapport, trust and building strong relationships. Much of my work has been responding to complex support needs for clients and families and helping them to navigate the disability support services.

I have received excellent feedback from the people I support due to my person centered and flexible approach. I am experienced, calm, friendly and supportive. I have a Diploma in Mental Health, current CPR and Working with Children Check.
