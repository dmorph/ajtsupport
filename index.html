What I do

My desire to become an Independent NDIS worker was to provide flexible, informed, personalised support that responds to both the client’s and their family’s needs. The NDIS offers a great opportunity for people and their families to take control over their supports and who supports them.

In our first meeting we will discuss your unique support needs and how I can assist you to meet them. I give professional personalised support focused on how to best address your needs.
